### Labeling Nodes:
- **Entity Types:** Use only the basic entity types: PERSON, ORGANIZATION, LOCATION, and DATE.
- **Miscellaneous Entities:** If an entity does not fit any of the predefined categories, label it as MISC.
- **Descriptive Properties:**
  For each entity, include all relevant descriptive attributes **explicitly mentioned in the text**.

  - The following examples illustrate the **minimum expected types of attributes**, but are **not exhaustive** (so include **any other explicitly stated properties**, beyond the examples):
    - For **PERSON**: profession, nationality, titles, or government/company positions.
    - For **LOCATION**: country, city, physical characteristics (e.g., size, capacity, type of place, region).
    - For **ORGANIZATION**: alternative names, type, descriptive qualifiers.
    - For **MISC**: any descriptors clarifying the entity.
  -**Important remark:** Do not include relationships between entities within the property list! Focus solely on entity-specific properties.

- **Stick to the text:** Do not infer, assume, or fabricate attributes. Only use what is explicitly present in the text.
- **Avoid Duplicate References:** Ensure that you do not provide multiple names referencing the same entity.
